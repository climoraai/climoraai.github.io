HabitSight Working Instructions

Follow these steps to set up HabitSight and start getting privacy-driven habit insights.

Step 01

Download & Install

Install HabitSight from the Google Play Store on your Android device.

Get it on Google Play

After installation, open HabitSight once before enabling permissions.

Step 02

Grant Usage Access

Allow Usage Access so HabitSight can generate usage-based behavior insights.

Settings Apps Special Access Usage Access Enable HabitSight

Before you continue

Important Notes

  • Android settings may vary depending on device model and Android version.
  • Use your Settings search bar and search “Usage access” if you cannot locate the permission.
  • Some devices may delay the first insight refresh after enabling permissions.
  • Certain battery optimisation systems may temporarily restrict background refresh activity.
Setup support

Troubleshooting

No insights appearing

Confirm Usage Access is enabled, then reopen HabitSight and allow a short refresh period.

Cannot find Usage Access

Open Android Settings and search for “Usage access” or “Special app access”.

Insights not refreshing

Check battery optimisation settings and make sure HabitSight is not being restricted.

Permission enabled but still empty

Disable and re-enable Usage Access, then restart the app to refresh local insight generation.

Privacy note

Privacy note: HabitSight is designed to minimize unnecessary data sharing. For full details about data handling and privacy practices, please read the Privacy Policy.
