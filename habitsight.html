HabitSight is built around a simple promise: your app usage insights should help you, without following you.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data that directly identifies you. Usage data is processed locally on your device.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used only within the app to generate insights. No tracking, profiling, or advertising.

3. Data Storage

All data remains stored locally on your device. No cloud storage or external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

No third-party tracking or advertising services are used. Essential platform services may be used for app functionality.

5. Permissions We Use

HabitSight requires Usage Access permission to analyze app usage patterns. This is processed entirely on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

HabitSight is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
