App Categories

Productivity Work, study, organization, and focus-related apps
Social Messaging and social communication apps
Entertainment Streaming, video, and media apps
Gaming Games and interactive entertainment
Other Utilities, browsers, and uncategorized apps

Efficiency Insights

Efficiency insights summarize focused and balanced digital behavior patterns. Productive balance, category balance, long sessions, entertainment-heavy usage, and late-night activity may influence the insight.

Digital Load Insights

Digital Load helps users recognize potentially intensive usage patterns, such as high total usage, late-night activity, long sessions, and entertainment-focused usage.

HabitSight insights are informational only and are designed to help users better understand general digital behavior patterns. Scores and labels are estimates based on usage trends and should not be interpreted as medical, psychological, or professional evaluations.
