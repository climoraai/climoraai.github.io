HabitSight FAQ

Answers to common questions about HabitSight, privacy, and usage.

Does HabitSight collect any personal data?

No. HabitSight does not collect or transmit personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

Does HabitSight track websites or URLs?

No. HabitSight only analyzes app usage data provided by Android's Usage Access API. It does not monitor websites, URLs, or browser activity.

Does HabitSight or the Latenora website use cookies or tracking?

HabitSight currently does not use advertising SDKs, behavioral advertising, or third-party profiling technologies.

Latenora websites currently operate without advertising cookies or third-party behavioral tracking technologies. If tracking or analytics technologies are introduced in future versions, the Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Does HabitSight require an account?

No account is required. HabitSight works offline and stores all data locally on your device.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete all HabitSight data at any time using the in-app “Purge All Data” option or by uninstalling the app.
