HabitSight FAQ

Answers to common questions about HabitSight, privacy, and usage.

Does HabitSight collect any personal data?

HabitSight is designed for local usage understanding. For full data-handling details, please refer to the Privacy Policy.

Does HabitSight track websites or URLs?

No. HabitSight only analyzes app usage data provided by Android's Usage Access API. It does not monitor websites, URLs, or browser activity.

Does HabitSight or the Latenora website use cookies or tracking?

HabitSight is designed to avoid unnecessary tracking. For complete details, please refer to the Privacy Policy.

Does HabitSight require an account?

HabitSight can be used without creating an account. For related privacy details, please refer to the Privacy Policy.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete all HabitSight data at any time using the in-app “Purge All Data” option or by uninstalling the app.
